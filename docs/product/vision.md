# 🎯 Product Vision

## 1. Core Mission Statement
**HourForge (StudyTracker)** is a premium, real-time study companion designed for students who value discipline over motivation. Our mission is to automate the cognitive overhead of study management, allowing students to focus 100% on mastery.

## 2. The Problem We're Solving
Modern students are overwhelmed by "Study Planning" but underwhelmed by "Study Action."
- **Forgetting Curve**: Most students study and then forget 70% of the material within 48 hours.
- **Tracking Friction**: Existing apps require manual logging that is often skipped or falsified.
- **Lone Wolf Syndrome**: Studying alone lacks an accountability loop.

## 3. The HourForge Solution
- **Discipline-First (The Companion)**: We move from "Manual Logging" to a "Live Study Timer" that removes the need for data entry.
- **Automated Mastery (The 2-4-7 Rule)**: The app intelligently schedules three critical revisions at the moment a study session ends.
- **Intelligent Feedback (The AI Mentor)**: Every session is analyzed by a "Brutally Honest" AI coach to detect patterns of neglect or burnout.

## 4. Competitive Advantage
- **Offline-First PWA**: Works as a native app on iOS/Android with sub-second performance.
- **Glassmorphism Aesthetic**: A stunning, modern UI that students *want* to interact with every day.
- **Low Friction**: Start study in 1 tap. Complete revision in 1 tap.

## 5. Our Ideal Student
The "High-Achiever" — someone in Science, Engineering, or Medicine who has a massive syllabus to cover and needs a rigid, scientifically-backed system to ensure long-term retention.
