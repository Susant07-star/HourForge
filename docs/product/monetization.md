# 💎 Monetization Strategy

## 1. Overview
HourForge (StudyTracker) utilizes a **Freemium** model designed to be high-value for free users while incentivizing serious students to upgrade for elite-level AI and analytics features.

## 2. Free vs. Premium Structure

### 🆓 The "Free" Student
Provides all core academic tracking to ensure every student can succeed.
- **Academic Tracking**: Unlimited study sessions and time logging.
- **Revision Engine**: Full 2-4-7 Spaced Repetition scheduling.
- **Offline PWA**: Complete local storage and basic Cloud Sync.
- **Basic Charts**: Study Hours and Subject Distribution trends.
- **Limited AI**: 3 "Psychology Insights" per day (1-line responses).

### 💎 The "Pro" Student (Premium)
Designed for the ultra-competitive student who needs maximal focus and growth.
- **Advanced AI Mentoring**: Unlimited Groq AI coaching calls.
- **Deep Insights**: Weekly "Performance Reports" with detailed subject-level deep-dives.
- **Focus Mode Pro**: Custom Pomodoro sounds and extended "Anti-Procrastination" timers.
- **Data Export**: CSV and PDF export for local backup and tutor reviews.
- **Priority Sync**: Realtime data sync with 0.1s latency.

## 3. Feature Gating
Gating is focused on the **Complexity** and **Intelligence** of the data, rather than the core utility.
- **Unlockable**: The "AI Rating" chart is a Pro-only visual motivator.
- **Unlockable**: Advanced "Peak Productivity" heatmap.

## 4. AI Usage Limits
To manage Groq / Llama API costs, limits are enforced at the account level:
- **Free**: 10 total AI tokens per day (Approx. 3 short coaching responses).
- **Pro**: High-cap or Unlimited tokens (Managed by monthly subscription).

## 5. Future Subscription Strategy
As the user base grows, the Pro tier will move from a "Lifetime" one-time payment to a **Subscription** model once multi-user social features (Leaderboards, Group Study) are introduced.

---
*Monetization is secondary to the core mission: HourForge must first be the best study companion in the world.*
