About HourForge

What is HourForge?

HourForge is a personal academic productivity app built to help students at every level study smarter, not harder. It combines spaced repetition revision, time logging, and AI-powered feedback — all in one place, completely offline-capable.

Features

Spaced Repetition
Auto-scheduled 2-4-7 day revision reminders to beat forgetting
Time Tracker
Log every study session with subject, start/end time and notes
AI Insights
Get brutally honest, data-driven feedback from an AI study coach
Progress Charts
Visual breakdown of daily hours, subject distribution, and revision rate
Student Profiles
Works for any student — configure your faculty, subjects and exam dates
Pomodoro Timer
Built-in focus timer with short and long break modes
Auto Backup
Link a local folder and auto-save your data — never lose progress
Streak Tracking
Daily study streaks to keep you accountable and consistent

How to Use

1
Set Up Your Profile
Tap the Profile icon → Choose your grade & faculty → Subjects auto-fill. Add exam dates for a countdown timer.
2
Log What You Study
On the Dashboard, add a session with the subject, chapter/topic, and date. The app auto-schedules revision reminders.
3
Do Your Revisions
Check the Due Today list daily. Green circles = done. Orange hourglass = revision due. Red = overdue!
4
Track Your Hours
Go to Time Tracker and log each activity with a start time, end time, and subject. This feeds the charts and AI.
5
Get AI Feedback
Visit AI Insights, enter your Groq API key (free), and generate a daily/weekly/monthly mentor analysis of your habits.
6
Backup Your Data
Use the Auto-Backup button to link a folder on your device. Data is saved automatically so you never lose it.
7
Add to Home Screen (as App)
iPhone (Safari): Tap the Share icon → "Add to Home Screen" → Tap Add. HourForge will appear as an app icon!

Android (Chrome): Tap the 3-dot menu → "Add to Home screen" or look for the install banner at the bottom of the screen.

Your Data — Safe & Portable

All your data is stored only on your device — no account, no cloud, fully private.
Export Data
Download a backup JSON — use the Export button in the header backup controls.
Import Data
Switching device? Use Import to restore from a previously exported file instantly.
Auto-Backup Folder
Link a local folder and the app auto-saves in the background — great for peace of mind.
